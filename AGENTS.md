# SubTrack

The goal of SubTrack is to provide a simple and efficient way to track and manage subscriptions. It allows users to keep track of their subscriptions, including the cost, renewal dates, and see the big picture spending metrics, like how much they are spending on subscriptions each month.

Currently, the project only serves me, but then engineering and design quality is high as it is open source. It will also serve as a portfolio project for me to showcase my skills in software development, design, and product management.
